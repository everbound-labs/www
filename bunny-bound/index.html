A deceptively simple puzzle game where split-second timing meets strategic planning. Guide your bunny through timed traps to collect carrots and climb the global leaderboard.

Coming soon to iOS and Android

About the Game

Bunny Bound is a timing-based puzzle game that's easy to pick up but tricky to master. Swipe to move your bunny across a grid of tiles, but watch out: traps appear and disappear on precise rhythms. Learn the patterns, time your hops perfectly, and race against the clock to earn 3 stars.

How to Play

Swipe to Hop

Simple swipe controls let you move your bunny up, down, left, or right, one tile at a time.

Avoid the Traps

Traps activate on set intervals. Study the rhythm, find your opening, and hop through safely.

Beat the Clock

Every level has time thresholds for 1, 2, and 3 stars. Can you perfect them all without losing a life?

Features

Timed Obstacle Courses

Traps pulse on and off in rhythmic patterns. Master the timing to navigate through increasingly complex obstacle courses.

Multiple Worlds

Journey through a variety of themed worlds, each with unique challenges and increasingly devious trap patterns.

Global Leaderboards

Compete against players worldwide. Your fastest completion times are ranked globally. Can you reach the top 10?

Star Rating System

Every level has 3-star objectives. Perfect your runs to collect them all and prove your mastery.

The Challenge Awaits

What starts as simple hop-and-collect puzzles quickly evolves into intricate timing challenges. Synchronized traps, narrow windows, and demanding time thresholds will test your reflexes and your patience.
