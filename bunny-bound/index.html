About the Game

Bunny Bound is a puzzle game where you guide a bunny through obstacle-filled worlds. Time your moves, dodge hazards, and race to the finish as fast as you can.

Challenge yourself to find the fastest route, then prove it on global leaderboards. Watch replays from top players to learn new strategies and shave off precious seconds.

Features

  • Precision movement through handcrafted worlds and levels
  • Global leaderboards: compete for the fastest time
  • Watch and learn from the best players' runs
  • Play offline, sync when you're back online
  • New worlds added regularly

For fans of timing and skill games like Geometry Dash, Crossy Road, or Super Hexagon, but where reading the pattern is just as important as the reflexes.
