Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 21, 2026
Last Updated: February 12, 2026

Everbound Labs LLC ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Bunny Bound mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

Account Information

When you create an account or link an existing account, we may collect:

  • Username (chosen by you)
  • Email address (if you link via email)

Communications

If you contact us for support, we collect the information you provide in your correspondence.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

Gameplay Data

  • Game progress, level completions, and scores
  • Leaderboard rankings and replay data
  • In-game purchases and virtual currency balances
  • Energy usage and refill times

Device Information

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system and version
  • Unique device identifiers
  • Language and region settings
  • Time zone

Usage Information

  • App feature usage and interactions
  • Session duration and frequency
  • Crash logs and performance data

1.3 Information Collected by Third Parties

Google AdMob (Advertising)

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements, including rewarded ads. AdMob may collect the following information:

  • Advertising ID (Google Advertising ID on Android, Identifier for Advertisers on iOS)
  • Device information (device type, operating system, language)
  • IP address
  • App usage data and ad interaction data
  • Approximate location (derived from IP address)

AdMob uses this information to serve ads, including personalized ads based on your interests. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy and How Google Uses Information from Sites or Apps that Use Our Services.

Payment Processors

In-app purchases are processed by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play). We do not collect or store your payment card information. We receive confirmation of purchases and transaction identifiers.

Operational Analytics

We use gameplay data (such as game progress and feature usage) for operational analytics. We do not use third-party analytics SDKs.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the App
  • Create and manage your account
  • Process in-app purchases and manage virtual currency
  • Display leaderboards and enable replay viewing
  • Send push notifications (such as energy refill alerts) if you opt in
  • Enable account recovery across devices
  • Deliver advertisements (including personalized ads if you consent)
  • Measure the effectiveness of advertisements
  • Respond to your comments, questions, and support requests
  • Monitor and analyze usage trends and preferences
  • Detect, prevent, and address technical issues, fraud, and cheating
  • Comply with legal obligations

3.1 Publicly Visible Information

  • Replays of leaderboard runs may be viewed by other players

3.2 Service Providers

We share information with third-party vendors who perform services on our behalf, including:

We use Google AdMob to serve advertisements in the App. AdMob is operated by Google LLC. When you view or interact with ads, AdMob collects certain information as described in Section 1.3.

  • AdMob may use cookies, device identifiers, and similar technologies

  • You can learn more at: https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543

3.4 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

3.5 Business Transfers

If we are involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

4. Advertising and Your Choices

4.1 Personalized Advertising

By default, AdMob may show you personalized advertisements based on your interests and activity. You can opt out of personalized advertising:

On iOS:

  • Go to Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking
  • Disable "Allow Apps to Request to Track"
  • Or deny tracking permission when prompted by the App

On Android:

  • Go to Settings > Google > Ads
  • Enable "Opt out of Ads Personalization"

4.2 Non-Personalized Ads

If you opt out of personalized advertising, you will still see ads, but they will not be based on your interests or activity.

4.3 Rewarded Ads

The App offers rewarded advertisements that allow you to earn virtual currency or energy by watching ads. Interaction with rewarded ads is optional.

5. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. If you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal information within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal purposes.

Leaderboard entries and replays may be retained for historical purposes even after account deletion.

Information collected by AdMob is retained according to Google's data retention policies.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6.1 Data Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach that affects your personal information, we will notify you in accordance with applicable law. Where required, we will notify you via email (if you have provided one) or through a notice in the App, and we will notify the relevant supervisory authorities within the timeframes required by law.

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@everboundlabs.com.

  • Legitimate Interests – for analytics, fraud prevention, and improving the App

7.5 Rights for California Residents

If you are a California resident, you have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including:

  • Right to know what personal information we collect
  • Right to delete your personal information
  • Right to opt out of the sale of personal information
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights

We do not sell personal information as defined by the CCPA. However, the use of advertising identifiers for targeted advertising may be considered a "sale" or "sharing" under California law. You can opt out using the methods described in Section 4.

To exercise your rights, contact us at privacy@everboundlabs.com.

7.6 Rights for Residents of Other US States

Residents of Virginia, Colorado, Connecticut, Utah, and other states with comprehensive privacy laws have similar rights to access, delete, and opt out of certain processing of their personal information. To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@everboundlabs.com.

7.7 Rights for Brazilian Residents (LGPD)

If you are located in Brazil, you have rights under the Lei Geral de Proteção de Dados (LGPD), including the right to access, correct, delete, and port your personal data, as well as the right to information about sharing and the right to revoke consent. To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@everboundlabs.com.
