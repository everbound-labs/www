Delete Account

You can request that your Bunny Bound account and all associated data be permanently deleted.

How to request deletion

  1. Open Bunny Bound and go to Settings
  2. Tap Email Support
  3. Change the subject line to "Delete My Account" and send the email

If you linked an email to your account, you can also email us directly at support@everboundlabs.com from that same email address with the subject "Delete My Account". We will verify that the email matches the one on file before processing the request.

What gets deleted

  • Your account and authentication data
  • Gameplay progress and best times
  • Leaderboard entries
  • Purchase history
  • Email address (if linked)
  • Push notification tokens

Timeline

Your data will be permanently deleted within 30 days of your request.
