About Us

Everbound Labs is a creative studio focused on building thoughtful, beautifully crafted games and interactive experiences.

Founded in 2025 and based in New York City, we love creating worlds that feel alive and memorable, blending together design, challenge, and emotion.

Our flagship title, Bunny Bound, is a precision-movement puzzle adventure about a small bunny finding a path through intricate worlds, one careful hop at a time.

At Everbound Labs, we build with patience and intention, aiming to create experiences that feel personal and worth returning to.
